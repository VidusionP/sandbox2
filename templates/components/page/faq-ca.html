Ghost Bond is a water-based glue meaning it can freeze if placed in low temperature areas. It should always be stored at temperatures above its freezing point for this reason. Expected handling times for all items are displayed on our website. When you select the color and grey percentage you are looking for, the amount currently in stock in each of our warehouses will be displayed above the color selection drop-downs. Below the drop-downs will have the expected handling time listed.
