Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

When it comes to hairpieces for women, they are a few options to choose from. There are hair toppers for women that help with concealing hair loss, thinning hair, or bald spots and patches. These hair toppers, also known as top pieces, tend to be smaller than wigs and can blend with the wearer’s natural hair to create an overall natural-looking appearance without anyone noticing the hairpiece.

There are also a variety of different wigs for women including full cap wigs, partial wigs, and medical wigs that come with beautiful, natural-looking Indian Remy, premium Chinese Remy, or European hair. Lastly, there are also hair integration systems that allow women to integrate their own hair into the system to achieve a realistic and undetectable appearance.

You have a couple of options as far as base materials for women’s hairpieces are concerned. For one, there’s monofilament bases which are known for being extremely durable and long-lasting. If a natural-looking, breathable and undetectable appearance is what you’re after, there’s also the option of lace bases. Skin bases may not be as natural, but they are realistic nonetheless and also come with the added benefit of being easy to attach and remove while offering the option to attach with glue or tape. Finally, there’s silicone bases in which no adhesive or clips are required whatsoever as these bases will securely grip and remain on your scalp.

It’s extremely important to know the correct cap size to get with your women’s hairpiece. If you get something smaller than required, it will slip off. If you get something larger, it won’t sit properly and will essentially give away that you’re wearing something.

Everyone is different as far as their level of hair loss and head size. With that being said, there are a number of different cap sizes available to choose from at Superhairpieces. They include the following:

  • 5’’x7’’ - small hairpieces
  • 6’’x8’’ - medium-sized hairpieces
  • 7’’x9’’ - large hairpieces

For anything larger, there are partial and full cap wigs for those who have total hair loss. It should be noted that these hairpiece sizes are adjustable and can be cut down further. Alternatively, you can place a custom order if you require a different size than what is offered by us.

All our women’s hairpieces come in one of four human hair types:

  • Regular hair - high quality Indian Remy human hair
  • Basic hair - more affordable version of our regular hair produced in a quicker timeline
  • Premium hair - luxury Chinese Remy human hair
  • European Remy human hair - comes in red, blonde, or brown tones

In general, most hairpiece wearers prefer to wear human hair since it’s real hair extracted from an individual. Human hair is not only natural-looking in appearance, but feels natural when it comes to touch and texture. While synthetic hair is much more natural today than it was in the past, it still lacks the overall feeling and look that human hair provides.

Additionally, human hair is heat-resistant unlike most synthetic hair, and so, it can be styled a lot more especially with heat styling products such as curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers. Synthetic hair is more durable as a whole and definitely more affordable, but the investment with human hair is worth it when it comes to achieving a natural-looking appearance.

Hair toppers are specifically meant for certain hair loss areas on the scalp such as thinning hair, bald spots, or bald patches. They are smaller than wigs and are meant to blend with the wearer’s natural hair in order to achieve a natural-looking appearance. And so, you are required to have a good amount of hair remaining in order to wear a hair topper. Finally, you will require either clips, tape or glue in order to attach a hair topper.

Wigs, on the other hand, are full cap for the most part which means they cover the entire scalp. They are ideally worn by those undergoing chemotherapy, have extreme or total hair loss, or women who simply want to try different hairstyles and colors. Women’s Wigs can also be attached without the need for clips, wig tape, or wig glue. Lastly, you don’t need to have any hair in order to wear a wig.

When choosing a hair color for your hairpiece, ideally you should look to choose something that is closest to your natural hair color. If you want to change things up a bit, we recommend going a maximum of two shades darker or lighter than your natural hair.

Some other things to consider include:

  • Matching your skin tone
  • Matching your eye color
  • Your age
  • The climate where you’re located
