FAQ
What is a hair system?
A hair system refers to a toupee, hair topper, or wig made of high-quality human hair that is used to conceal hair loss, thinning hair, or bald areas.
What is an installation?
This process involves the attachment of your new hair system onto your scalp. It includes customizing the base size, styling the hair on the system, and blending it with your natural hair for seamless coverage.
What is maintenance?
Hair systems need maintenance every 3-4 weeks to remove product build-up, increase longevity, and enhance their appearance. Removing, washing, reinstalling them, and trimming your natural hair to blend well are all parts of the process.
Do you require color?
We have a wide variety of hair system colors; however, sometimes you need to get them colored by our professionals to match them with your exact natural hair shade, keeping in mind the gray coverage too.
