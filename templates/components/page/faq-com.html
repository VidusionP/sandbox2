Water-based glues, like Safe Grip or Ghost Bond. Use a brush to apply a thin, even layer and let it dry for exactly seven minutes until it dries clear.

Ghost Bond is a water-based glue meaning it can freeze if placed in low temperature areas. For this reason, it should always be stored at temperatures above its freezing point. This is also why we advise against having Ghost Bond delivered in the winter time if you live in a cold area, as the Ghost Bond might freeze and crack the bottle.

You should avoid putting acrylic-based glues on lace or monofilament material as it can break down the hair and will cause shedding.
